# USA Housing Market Analysis

## Business Question
Where does a buyer's money go furtherest and what drives price premium

## Key Findings
1. Hawaii average price is 120.9% above the national average, meaning a large share of US buyers are effectively priced out of this market
2. Smaller homes cost approxiamtely 81.8% less per square foot than large-sized homes, showing a strong size-based premium for compact housing
3. Each bedroom up to 5 beds adds approximately $192k-$333k, after which returns drop and become inconsistent(approximately $269k-$299k), showing diminishing returns
